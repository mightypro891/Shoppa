# **App Name**: Naija Shoppa

## Core Features:

- Browse Products: Browse available Nigerian foodstuffs.
- Shopping Cart: Add items to a shopping cart.
- Checkout Process: Proceed to checkout and confirm order details.
- Order Tracking: Track order status updates.
- Recipe Suggestions: AI-powered recommendations tool that suggests Nigerian recipes based on the items in your cart.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Vibrant Orange (#FF7F50) to represent the warmth and richness of Nigerian culture and food.
- Background color: Light Beige (#F5F5DC), a neutral and inviting backdrop.
- Accent color: Earthy Brown (#A0522D) for highlights, buttons, and important information, adding a touch of groundedness and authenticity.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif that provides a balance of modernity and readability.
- Use clear, simple icons representing various food categories and actions, ensuring ease of navigation.
- Clean and intuitive layout, focusing on product presentation and easy navigation for students.
